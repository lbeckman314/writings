date: 2017-10-10

```
Pinpricks of lights spin past
    in the lasting nightime bare
while freinds gather here for
    a brief sojourn affair
& Rose-tipped petal flames do sit so very far
    from the restless sky above looking down
        upon them all.

& then they fall.
```
